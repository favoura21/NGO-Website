About Our Training Programs

Participants can apply to train for one of the three tracks: information technology, media production, or renewable energy. The training will typically last six months and will have two pathways: the Entrepreneur Track and the Employee Track. The former will provide participants with the prerequisite skills to become entrepreneurs, while the latter will equip participants with advanced, employable skills for employment in the industry.


information technology

In today's digital world, IT skills are essential. Our program covers computer literacy, software applications, web development, and digital tools that prepare participants for careers in technology, entrepreneurship, and beyond.

After the Training

After completing the training, individuals in the entrepreneurial pathway can begin their businesses. iSABI may provide some non-financial support to early entrepreneurs, such as mentorship, networking opportunities, and access to resources. We are committed to helping our graduates succeed in their entrepreneurial ventures. For those in the employee pathway, iSABI will support you in finding employment. We work with selected job recruiting agencies to ensure that our trainees are equipped with the skills and competencies sought by prospective employers.

Over 1,000 individuals trained across 5 communities.
