Funding

Together, We Can Fund a Better Future

iSABi relies on donations and grants from individuals, companies, and foundations. Grants received for training will be applied as scholarships to support qualified applicants.

Donate Now

Paying for training

Training fees are funded from either of these two sources: scholarship or self-funding. iSABI will offer scholarships in the form of full or partial fee remission. Full scholarships cover tuition fees, accommodation, and food. Partial scholarships cover only a percentage of the tuition fee. It does not include accommodation and feeding. Self-funded students pay the total cost of the training by themselves. Self-funding applicants may be eligible for a tuition fee discount, as determined by iSABI.
