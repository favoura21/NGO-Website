Welcome to iSABI

iSABI is an international, not-for-profit, non-governmental organisation that champions sustainable youth empowerment and job creation on the African continent.We firmly believe in the transformative power of knowledge.

Community Impact

When young people are equipped with technical expertise and entrepreneurial skills, they can catalyze positive change. This belief is encapsulated in the word 'iSABI', which, in Nigerian Pidgin English, translates to 'I have the skills'.

Join Our Mission

At iSABI, our establishment is a testament to our unwavering commitment to human dignity and the fair distribution of wealth. We operate within the social context of empowering young people to create sustainable jobs, ensuring that our values are reflected in all our endeavours.
