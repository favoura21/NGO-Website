About Us

We Help People In Need Around The World

iSABI is an international, not-for-profit, non-governmental organisation that promotes and supports sustainable youth empowerment and job creation on the African continent. iSABI recognises that knowledge is power. When the young are empowered with technical knowledge and entrepreneurial skills, they can become drivers of positive change. iSABI's emphasis on transformational knowledge is reflected in the word "iSABI," which, in Nigerian Pidgin English, can be translated to mean "I have the skills."

Micheal Akpoghiran, Founder

About Us

Our Mission

Our mission is to empower young people to become drivers of positive social change through sustainable job creation initiatives.

Our Vision

In iSABI, we envision an African society where young people are not just seen, but recognised as invaluable assets. We empower them to harness their best capabilities and develop their potential, fostering a future full of promise and opportunity.

Our Objectives

  • To contribute to the reduction of poverty through providing access to technical and entrepreneurial training that leads to job creation.
  • To promote structures, practices and processes that lead to the equitable distribution of wealth and work.
  • To strengthen the capacities of individuals, organisations and communities that lead to the empowerment of the young.
  • To source and share information about the state of affairs in youth unemployment to inform and influence public policy.

Our Core Values: I CAN

  • Integrity:To adhere to the principles that honour the best in us.
  • Confidence: To harness our capacity to create sustainable, positive change.
  • Aspiration: To believe in becoming better citizens and employment game-changers.
  • Neighbourliness: To deploy our competencies to care for ourselves and the environment.
